FEATURES

Virtual Labs

Virtual labs are simulated learning environments that allow you to complete laboratory experiments online and explore concepts and theories without stepping into a physical science lab.

  • Simulator
  • Video Tutorial
  • Theory
  • Viva-Voce
  • Download Record

A Play-Ground For You

It's like playing a video game in a controlled environment. You will spend a lot of time here to understand and excel in the experiment.

  • You perform the experiment & record the observations.
  • You do all the calculations & submit results.
  • The Simulator validates your results and notifies you.
  • The Simulator stores all your experiment data for record generation.

A Video Guide To Assist You

A video tutorial explains how to perform the experiment on PhyLab application.

  • Explains what the aim of the experiment is.
  • Explains how to perform the experiment & record observations.
  • Explains how to calculate the results and submit.
  • Explains how to generate PDF record.

A Textual Guide

Provides the text-based approach to the concept behind the experiment.

  • Explains the concept and formulae to use.
  • Explains what apparatus are needed.
  • Explains precautions to take while doing experiment in the real lab.

Tool To Test Your Knowledge

Provides five randomly selected multiple choice questions to test your knowledge.

A Proof For You & Lecturer

Generates a PDF report of your completed experiment.

  • Generate any number of times, no limit.
  • You are free to print it & show to your lecturer as a proof.
  • You can attach it to your record.
